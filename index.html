About Rory Butler Beats

Rory Butler is a skilled and experienced music producer who has worked with a number of artists through personal connections as well as his online Beat Store

Services and Offers

Rory Butler Beats offers a range of varied beats for artists ranging across styles such as Hip Hop, Pop, Alternative, Emo and more.

  • Buy 2 Get 1 Free

    Every beat on the Rory Butler Beat Store is under a Buy 2 Get 1 Free offer at all times. If you buy any two beat leases, you will get a third for free.

    Note: This is only for Beat Leases and does not include Exclusive agreements. Make contact for negotiations on Exclusive pricing.

  • Vocal Mixing Included

    Any beat that is purchased from Rory Butler Beats will benefit from an included vocal mix. When you buy your beat, write and record your vocals over it, you will get that song mixed for no extra cost

    This is included at every price point, so whether you have bought an MP3 lease, or an Exclusive agreement, you get a vocal mix over that beat for free!

    This offer is only for beats purchased from Rory Butler Beats, but mixing services are offered at a cost for any other tracks.

  • Custom Beats

    Rory Butler Beats isn't just about posting online - working with artists to achieve their perfect vision is a huge part of the business.

    With no styles off-limits, but a heavy focus on Hip Hop, Pop, Alternative and associated styles, any music production enquiries to reach your vision can be met.

    Static pricing is not currently available, but make contact to discuss your custom beat and to organise a quote.

  • Audio Engineering

    With a passion for mixing and a strife for perfection, any audio engineering enquiries are welcomed.

    Having studied the craft of mixing greatly, Rory is a cut above many other beatmakers who offer mixing.

    With a deep understanding of audio engineering and a wealth of knowledge of how to mix, you won't be disappointed with your results.
